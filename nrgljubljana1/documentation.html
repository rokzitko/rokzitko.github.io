Documentation¶

NRG Ljubljana solves quantum impurity problems such as the single-impurity Anderson model (SIAM), described by the following Hamiltonian:

\[\begin{split}H &= \sum_{k\sigma} \epsilon_k c^\dagger_{k\sigma} c_{k\sigma} + \sum_\sigma \epsilon_d d^\dagger_\sigma d_\sigma + U n_\uparrow n_\downarrow \\ &+ \sum_{k\sigma} \left( V_k c^\dagger_{k\sigma} d_\sigma + \text{H.c.} \right)\end{split}\]

with \(n_\sigma=d^\dagger_\sigma d_\sigma\). Here c are conduction-band operators, while d are impurity operators.
